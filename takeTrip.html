About Mysore

Mysore the Heritage city of India which is present in the southern part of Karnataka. The place ruled by the Wodeyars dynasty. The place famous for "palace",'"MysorePak", "Mysore Mallige", "Mysore Silk" etc. It attracts tourists from every part of the county and abroad because of it's rich diversity and culture. It is one of the cleanest city in India according recent survey of swaccha bharath mission. The climate is almost similar throught year. Transportation facility is good. Recently they started "Trin Trin" cycles for tourists. The place is connected to other places such as Coorg, Ooty, Bangalore, Sri Rangapatna and more. The land of 5 and 3 star hotels. Visit mysore it's going to be amazing experience.
